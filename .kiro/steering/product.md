# Product Overview

Durrah is a property management platform for the Middle Eastern market that enables users to search, view, book, and manage properties across multiple categories: Rent, Buy, Shops, and Activities.

## User Roles

- **Guest**: Books properties for temporary stays, manages bookings, submits maintenance tickets
- **Investor**: Owns properties, manages bookings, tracks revenue and occupancy metrics
- **Owner**: Owns properties, manages maintenance operations and property information

## Core Features

- Multi-language support (Arabic RTL / English LTR) with client-side switching
- Phone/OTP authentication via Firebase
- Property search with advanced filtering (type, price, location, amenities, beach view)
- Booking management with date selection, pricing calculation, and cancellation policies
- Secure payment processing through Stripe
- Interactive maps via Mapbox for property locations
- Maintenance ticket system with categories, priorities, and photo attachments
- Review and rating system for properties
- Role-specific dashboards with analytics and management tools
- Email and in-app notifications for booking and ticket status changes

## Business Categories

- **Rent**: Apartments, villas, townhouses for short/long-term rental
- **Buy**: Properties available for purchase
- **Shops**: Commercial retail and office spaces for lease
- **Activities**: Bookable experiences and venues

## Key Differentiators

- Dual-role support for property owners (Investor for revenue tracking, Owner for maintenance)
- Comprehensive maintenance ticket workflow integrated with property management
- Multi-category platform (residential, commercial, activities) in one system
- Full Arabic language support with proper RTL layout
